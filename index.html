Cancel Culture: A Modern Phenomenon

Cancel Culture has become a strong phenomena in the Philippines, particularly in the Showbiz Industry, among politicians and other well-known public figures. This results in causing the public figure a lot of harm including mental health issues, damaged career prospects, and bad reputation. This website explores the complex dynamics of cancel culture, examining its origins, impacts, and implications for our society. Through this, we aim to spread awareness towards the netizens and overall lessen the negativity cancel culture brings to us in the internet.
