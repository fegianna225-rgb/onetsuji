Meet The TsuMils

We are six students who have come together to inform and educate Filipino netizens on the complex topic of Cancel Culture. We are here to show the good and the bad of cancel culture, while equipping netizens with the critical thinking and logical analysis required to properly assess, address, and understand these issues.

Our Mission

At End The Cancel Culture, we aim to emphasize Cancel Culture as a "double-edged sword" that can hurt or protect. Through education, we hope to equip young, impressionable Filipino netizens with the skills to protect their community.

Our Vision

We envision the Filipino cyberspace to be free from the injustice and prejudice of Cancel Culture by the formation of netizens who approach problems with critical-thinking and logical reasoning, who choose to do what is right and uplift members of society.

The Story Behind Our Logo

Sword of Justice

Represents the double-edged nature of cancel culture–a symbol of accountability that can also harm when misused.

Balance Scale

Stands for fairness and empathy, reminding us to weigh both sides before passing judgment.

ETC Typography

The sword forms the letter T in ETC, uniting the symbol of justice with our name — End the Cancel Culture and showing that justice is at the core of our advocacy to end cancel culture.
