Meet The TsuMils

We are six students who have come together to make a stand against the digital injustice of Cancel Culture. While cancel culture can be generally helpful, we believe that its negative effects outweigh the pros. Together, we hope to inform netizens about the negative effects of cancel culture and provide tips for addressing this issue.

Our Mission

At End The Cancel Culture, we aim to educate Filipino netizens about the positive and negative effects of cancel culture. By fostering understanding and awareness, we hope to encourage accountability that uplifts rather than destroys.

Our Vision

We envision the Filipino cyberspace to be free from the injustice and prejudice of Cancel Culture by the formation of netizens who approach problems with critical-thinking and logical reasoning, who choose to do what is right and uplift members of society.
